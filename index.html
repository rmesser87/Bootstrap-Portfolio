I was born in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. My passion in wildlife and the environment led me to pursue a B.S. in Conservation Biology from LSU. Following graduation, I took several temporary Biologist positions in Louisiana, Tennessee, Illinois, Iowa, Wisconsin, North Dakota, and Alaska. Those years as a temporary biologist, although fulfilling, made it clear to me that to advance my career I needed to advance my education.

I pursued a master's degree in Geography- Applied GIS from Sam Houston State University. Following graduation from SHSU, I worked as a Fur Biologist for the Louisiana Department of Wildlife and Fisheries. Wanting more financial stability I moved to Houston to work for an environmental consulting company. Not long after working at the consulting company, I was tasked with creating a GIS department. Now here I am again, wanted to advance my education and career as a developer.
